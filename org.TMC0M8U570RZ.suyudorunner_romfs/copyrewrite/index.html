CopyRewrite Proposal

By Benjamin Mickle

To President Donald Trump and the United States Congress:

Hello, this is Benjamin Mickle, resident of Illinois and lead developer of libTmcNnDetect and ndexec.
The reason I developed this DRM is to expose the problems with Digital Millennium Copyright Act and to propose a replacement which I have dubbed “CopyRewrite Act” or CRA for short.
DMCA has several unconstitutional components to it, such as the 1st-Amendment-violating Title 17 § 1201,
the very law which my DRM exploits and several other DRM solutions exploit, and the 5th- and 8th-Amendment-violating way in which DMCA is enforced.

The reason I am targeting Nintendo Co., LTD with the design of my DRM is because they are the epitome of corporations that abuse these unconstitutional laws.
In 1985, Nintendo Co., LTD filed a patent for the very first game console DRM chip known as 10NES or CIC.
DMCA’s enactment in 1998 allowed Nintendo Co., LTD to basically abuse this patent in their lawsuits,
framing everything in terms of Title 17 § 1201 and with DMCA extending the life of a copyright to life of holder + 70 years, that copyright won’t expire until 2089, 70 years after John Kirby’s death.
This means these people will be able to continue abusing this one patent to bully individuals who can’t afford to fight back until probably after I’m dead.

The proposed solution? Break all connections with WIPO and form a new copyright system with fairness to the individuals in mind. A list of changes:

  • Abandonware shall become public domain.
  • Copyrights shall have a limit of 30 years of life.
  • All transformative works shall be fair use regardless of whether they are SFW or NSFW and regardless of form (Edit, mod, fanart, fangame, fanfic, etc.)
  • Circumvention without piracy or online cheating shall be exempt from anti-circumvention laws.
  • The penalty for violating fair use shall be much greater than the penalty for infringing on an intellectual property.
  • Due process in takedowns shall be a constitutional right.
  • Corporation legal team size shall be limited to three individuals to make the alleged infringer stand a chance.
  • Arbitration agreements shall be banned.
  • Artificial intelligence shall be banned from detecting copyright infringement.
